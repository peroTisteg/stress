Understanding Stress

Stress results from various pressures such as school demands, relationship issues, and social media. It manifests as physical, emotional, or mental strain.

Facts and Statistics

  • Approximately 70% of students report feeling stressed about exams.
  • Social media usage has been linked to increased anxiety and depression.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent headaches or muscle tension.
  • Difficulty concentrating or sleeping.
  • Feelings of being overwhelmed or anxious.

Identifying Stress

Look for signs like changes in behavior, mood swings, or withdrawal from social activities. Early identification can help in managing stress effectively.
